Privacy Policy — Dino Hop: Endless Island Run

Studio Mars · Package com.studiomars.dino
Effective date: 14 July 2026

This policy explains what information Dino Hop: Endless Island Run (the "Game") handles, and how. The Game is intended for a general audience, including children. We have designed it so that it collects no personal information from any player.

We do not collect personal information

The Game has no accounts, no sign-in, and no user profiles. We do not ask for, and do not collect, names, email addresses, phone numbers, photographs, contacts, location, or any other personal information.

Game progress is stored on your device

Your coins, XP, levels, upgrades, high scores, daily rewards, and settings are saved locally on your device. This information stays on the device, is not transmitted to us, and is deleted when you uninstall the Game.

Advertising

The Game shows rewarded video ads only, served by Google AdMob. These ads:

  • are never shown automatically — an ad plays only after you tap a button that is clearly labelled "Ad";
  • are always optional; you can play the entire Game without watching any ad;
  • are restricted to a "G" (General audiences) maximum content rating.

Every ad request from the Game is tagged for child-directed treatment under the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). This means the ads you see are non-personalized: they are not based on your interests, browsing history, or past behaviour, and no interest-based advertising or remarketing is used.

The Game does not collect or transmit your device's Advertising ID (AAID). The Android advertising-ID permission has been removed from the app.

Google may process a limited amount of technical, non-personal information — such as coarse device type, operating-system version, and the ad request itself — in order to deliver an ad, cap how often the same ad is shown, and prevent ad fraud. Google's handling of this data is described in the Google Privacy & Terms and in the AdMob Families policy guidance.

No purchases

The Game contains no in-app purchases. We do not collect or process any payment information.

No analytics or tracking

The Game does not include analytics, advertising-attribution, or third-party tracking software. We do not build profiles of players and we do not track players across apps or websites.

No data sharing or sale

We do not sell, rent, or share any player information, because we do not collect any.

Children's privacy

The Game's target audience includes children. We comply with the Google Play Families Policy, COPPA, and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as they apply to children. Because the Game collects no personal information and serves only non-personalized, G-rated ads, no parental consent is required for data collection — there is none to consent to.

If you are a parent or guardian and you have a question about the Game, please contact us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

If the Game's data practices change, we will update this page and revise the effective date above. Significant changes will also be reflected in the Game's Google Play listing.
